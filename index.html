Almost one year after the vinyl release in the real world, we now bring you the digital release for download.

De ware Boembep 12-inch was released on Dutch Gems Records in a limited edition of 200 copies to celebtrate 30 years of music-making, and to honour Ron 'Kese' Doove. A former member who had recently passed away.

We've also included all the artwork and stories that were included in the album. To top of this digital party there's the previously unreleased video of the release pary, wich was held at Nowas Record store.

If you missed all this before, you can still get a real copy of the record directly from Dutch Gems Records
