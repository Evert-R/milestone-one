Chill Oud Posse

DG002 Digital Release

Almost one year after the vinyl release in the physical world, we now present to you the digital release in cyber-space.

De ware Boembep 12-inch was released on Dutch Gems Records in a limited edition of 200 copies to both celebrate the fact that we started making hip-hop 30 years ago, as well as to honour Ron 'Kese' Doove. A former member who had recently passed away.

On this website you will find everything that was included in this collectors item, as well as the oportunity to buy the original 12" on vinyl.
